Harness the power of remote monitoring

Fix issues, Save power and save money

Monitoring engine parameters helps in early diagnosis of developing problems and fixing issues before they lead to unpleasant consequences raises the reliability of both emergency systems and primary power systems. At Eyesline Telematics, we build wireless remote generator monitoring systems for on-site power, standby power and phone tower site monitoring applications. You can remotely determine the status of your generators round the clock from anywhere, thereby fixing issues before they turn into severe problems and cut-down the need for costly service and on-site visits.

Our state-of-the-art system can interface with intelligent generator controllers or connect to sensors fitted to simple machines to track engine operations and identify any issues or failures.
